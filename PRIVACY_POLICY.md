# Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the AJP App handles user data.

---

## Information Collection
The App may collect the following information when required for functionality:
- Device information (Android version, device model)
- App usage data
- User-provided data (if login or forms are used)

The App does NOT collect sensitive personal data unless explicitly stated.

---

## Permissions
The App may request access to:
- Storage (for file upload/download)
- Internet access
- Notifications (optional)

Permissions are used strictly for app functionality.

---

## Data Usage
Collected data is used only to:
- Improve app performance
- Fix bugs
- Enhance user experience

Data is NOT sold or shared with third parties.

---

## Data Security
Reasonable measures are taken to protect user data.
However, no system can guarantee 100% security.

---

## Children's Privacy
This app is intended for students.
No knowingly collected data from children under applicable laws is misused.

---

## Changes to This Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated in future releases.
Continued use of the app means acceptance of the updated policy.

---

## Contact
